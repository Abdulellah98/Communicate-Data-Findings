Investigation Overview¶

In the investigation, I have look at the characteristics of the loans that could be used in proving the relationship between the loan amount and the interest rate (BorrowerRate) after July 2009. The main focus was on the original loan amount, borrower's Prosper rating, loan term, Stated Monthly Income, and MonthlyLoanPayment.

Dataset Overview¶

The Prosper Loan data is about loans since 2005 it's contains apporixametly 114,000 loans and 81 attributes. I have selected the data after July 2009 since the original data have a lot of missing values. It contains about 85,000 loans and I have choice 16 varibales such as Term, BorrowerAPR, BorrowerRate, LoanOriginalAmount, and MonthlyLoanPayment, etc.
